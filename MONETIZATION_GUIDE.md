## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues:
1. **"Why pay when it's free?"**
   - Solution: Clear value prop (videos, community, cert)
   - Keep free tier valuable but limited

2. **Competition from free resources**
   - Solution: Emphasize structure, community, support
   - Focus on "production-ready" positioning

3. **High support burden**
   - Solution: Community-first support, FAQ, troubleshooting guide
   - Limit 1-on-1 support to Pro tier

4. **Content becomes outdated**
   - Solution: Quarterly updates, version tracking
   - Pro tier gets updates included
